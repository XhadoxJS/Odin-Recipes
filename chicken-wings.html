Air Fried Buffalo Wings


Description

The following is a recipe for an easy to prepare and cook buffalo wings meal. There are few ingredients and prep time is minimal. Enjoy your fresh buffalo wings!


Ingredients

  • 2.5 lbs Chicken Wings
  • 1 tblspoon Olive Oil
  • 2/3 cups Cayenne Pepper Sauce
  • 1/2 cup Butter
  • 2 tblspoons Vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat an fryer to 360 degrees F (180 degrees C).
  2. Place wings in a large bowl, drizzle oil, and massage until chicken is evenly coated. Arrange 1/2 of the wings in a single layer in the air fryer basket.
  3. While the second batch is cooking, combine hot pepper sauce, butter, vinegar, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper in a small saucepan over medium heat. Stir and keep warm until wings are cooked.
  4. Poor sauce over cooked wings and toss to coat.
